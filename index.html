Case Studies

Canada Council for the Arts Redesign Project

Drawing from User Insights & Testing, a high-fidelity prototype of the responsive web redesign was developed & refined for the Canada Council for the Arts. A comprehensive new style guide was established to maintain consistency and cohesion throughout the redesigned platform.

Abbey Cat Adoptions Redesign Project

Valuable insights from users highlighted deficiencies in Abbey Cat Adoptions' outdated and unresponsive web design, prompting a comprehensive overhaul. This endeavour resulted in the creation of a responsive high-fidelity prototype, complemented a new style guide. Together, these efforts ensured the maintenance of consistency and cohesion across the revitalized platform.
